About C-SoC
Another interesting initiative taken by CESS to introduce open source culture to the coders. Summer of Code focuses on bringing up the geeky coders to work together on open source projects for furnishing/enhancing their technical skills. SOC, a learning event is for every student who has keen interest in coding and is willing to contribute to society.




The Game Plan

It's always good to know about the event's schedule, so you can do some handy preparations before the event.

May 24
Registration Starts. Teams will start prepairing their Proposals.
June 6

June 10
Teams whose proposals are accepted will start developing their projects.
July 30
Results are declared and winning products are faciliated with prizes. Happy Ending with closing notes.
