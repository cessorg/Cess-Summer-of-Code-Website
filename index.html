Rules and Regulations
  • In order to participate you must register as early as possible before deadline by filling details and paying fees.
  • One project could be allocated to maximum of 3 teams.
  • We will send you confirmation mail with in 12 hours after which you can start proposal submission by mailing us.
  • The projects will be allocated on the basis of first come first serve and proposal quality.
  • In case project slots are already full or the proposal is not upto mark we will inform you within 12 hrs for re-choosing project or resubmission of proposal.
  • The selected project proposal list would be updated regularly.
  • You can submit your own ideas as well.
  • Weightage would be given on the basis of project difficulty, commits, documentation, readability, etc while evaluating projects.

In case of any doubts feel free to reach us by contacts provided below.
About C-SoC
Another interesting initiative taken by CESS to introduce open source culture to the coders. Summer of Code focuses on bringing up the geeky coders to work together on open source projects for furnishing/enhancing their technical skills. SOC, a learning event is for every student who has keen interest in coding and is willing to contribute to society.
